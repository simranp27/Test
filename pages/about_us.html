About Us

        Bibliotribe is a sepcial platform created by Simran, Monika and Vidya for geeks, book-lovers, nerds and everyone else who breathes in the name of books. The motive is to bring them all together from around the globe to enhance the feel of a community that are dedicated to self-development, literature and philosophy.

        Simran, Monika and Vidya are currently pursuing B.E. in Information Technology department. The Bibliotribe is an outcome of their three years of Engineering in St. John College of Engineering, Palghar.

        The Bibliotribe is a mimic of a bookstore cafe. The Bibliotribe allows you to download the desired books, novels for offline reading, so that the reader can read at its own pace. It also gives an opportunity to share their views via blogs. The seeker may read as well as write blogs. thus, mimicking a bookstore cafe where people freely and willingly communicate.
